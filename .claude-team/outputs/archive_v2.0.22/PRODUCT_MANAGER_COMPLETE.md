## Pricing Recommendation

### Tier Structure

#### 1. Free Tier - "CloudSync Starter"
- **Price:** $0
- **Features:**
  - 2 cloud connections
  - Basic sync (manual only)
  - 5GB monthly transfer limit
  - Community support
- **Purpose:** Lead generation, product experience

#### 2. Pro Tier - "CloudSync Pro"
- **Price:** $9.99/month or $99/year (17% discount)
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited cloud connections
  - All 42+ providers
  - Scheduled sync (hourly/daily/weekly)
  - Transfer preview & optimization
  - End-to-end encryption
  - Priority support (AI-powered)
  - No transfer limits
- **Target:** Power users, freelancers, creators

#### 3. Team Tier - "CloudSync Team"
- **Price:** $19.99/user/month (annual only)
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Pro
  - Team management dashboard
  - Shared team connections
  - Audit logs
  - SSO integration (Phase 2)
  - SLA guarantee
- **Target:** Small teams, agencies

#### 4. Enterprise Tier (Future)
- **Price:** Custom ($50k+ annual contracts)
- **Features:** Compliance, dedicated support, custom integrations
- **Target:** Large organizations (Year 3+)

### Pricing Rationale
- **$99/year** positions above CloudMounter ($30) but below ExpanDrive ($1,188)
- Monthly option captures commitment-phobic users
- Team pricing scales revenue per organization
- Matches SaaS benchmarks: 9-10% free-to-paid conversion expected
