# CloudSync Ultra Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Introduction

CloudSync Ultra ("we", "our", "us", or the "App") is developed by [Company Name] and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CloudSync Ultra handles your information when you use our macOS application.

CloudSync Ultra is designed with privacy in mind. We operate on a local-first principle, meaning your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use CloudSync Ultra, you may provide:

- **Cloud Provider Credentials**: Authentication tokens and credentials for services like Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and other supported cloud providers
- **App Preferences and Settings**: Your configuration choices, sync preferences, and application settings
- **File Selection Information**: Which files and folders you choose to sync

### Information Collected Automatically

CloudSync Ultra may collect:

- **Crash Reports**: Technical information about app crashes (stored locally only, optional sharing)
- **Performance Metrics**: Local performance data to optimize sync operations (stored on device only)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

CloudSync Ultra does NOT collect:

- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Your files' contents
- Browsing history
- Location data
- Contact information
- Marketing data

## How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

- **Provide Sync Functionality**: Enable synchronization between your device and cloud storage providers
- **Store Your Preferences**: Maintain your app settings and sync configurations
- **Improve App Stability**: Use crash reports to fix bugs and improve reliability (only if you choose to share)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All data is stored locally on your macOS device
- Cloud provider credentials are securely stored in macOS Keychain
- Sync configurations are stored in app-specific directories with appropriate file permissions
- No data is transmitted to our servers

### Security Measures

- We use macOS Keychain Services for secure credential storage
- The app follows macOS security best practices
- Optional encryption for synced files using rclone's encryption features
- All cloud provider communications use secure HTTPS connections

## Third-Party Services

CloudSync Ultra interacts with:

### Cloud Storage Providers
Your chosen cloud providers (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, etc.) have their own privacy policies and terms of service. When you authenticate with these services, you are subject to their privacy practices for data stored on their platforms.

### rclone
CloudSync Ultra uses rclone, an open-source command line program, as its sync engine. rclone processes data locally on your device and does not collect or transmit data to external servers.

### Apple Services
- **App Store**: For app distribution and updates
- **macOS System Services**: For Keychain access and system integration

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have full control over your data:

- **View Your Data**: All settings and configurations are accessible within the app
- **Delete Your Data**: You can remove cloud provider connections, clear sync history, and delete all app data
- **Export Your Data**: Sync configurations can be exported for backup purposes
- **Modify Settings**: Change your preferences at any time

### Data Deletion
To completely remove CloudSync Ultra data:
1. Remove cloud provider connections in the app
2. Delete the app from your Applications folder
3. Remove app preferences from ~/Library/Preferences/
4. Remove Keychain entries via Keychain Access app

## Children's Privacy

CloudSync Ultra is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to the app, please contact us.

## International Data Transfers

Since CloudSync Ultra operates locally, we do not transfer your data internationally. However, your cloud storage providers may store your synced files in various locations according to their policies.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information:

- Right to delete personal information

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:
- **Legal Basis**: We process data based on legitimate interests (providing app functionality)
- **Data Portability**: You can export your sync configurations
- **Right to Erasure**: You can delete all app data
- **Right to Object**: You can stop using the app at any time
